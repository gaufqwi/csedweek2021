Introduction

Hi there. Welcome to whatever this is.

Actually, I can tell you what this is. I taught middle school for 20 years. For the last five years of that my absolute favorite time of year was Computer Science Education Week. The Hour of Code was always fun, of course, but I also enjoyed providing trivia questions and other activities for our students and faculty. Since I'm not in the classroom anymore I don't get to do that now and that makes me sad. So decided to make my own fun and put up some CSEdWeek activities right here.

To be a little more specific, my goal for this little project is to offer computer science trivia questions every day as well as a series of tutorials on creating a simple platformer type game with Javascript. If I can think of anything else, then I'll share that too.
