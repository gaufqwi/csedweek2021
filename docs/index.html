Introduction

Welcome. I taught middle school for 20 years. For the last five years of that my absolute favorite time of year was Computer Science Education Week. The Hour of Code was always fun, of course, but I also enjoyed providing trivia questions and other activities for our students and faculty. Since I'm not in the classroom anymore I don't get to do that now and that makes me sad. So decided to make my own fun and put up some CSEdWeek activities right here.

Each day I plan on offering a set of computer science trivia questions along with some other computer science education content. It might be a set of links or a lesson plan or a short text or video tutorial. Last year I did a four day course on creating a platformer-type game that runs in your web browser, but this year I'm going to keep things a little more open ended. Check back every day to see what's new.
